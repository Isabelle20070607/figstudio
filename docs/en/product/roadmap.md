## Planning Model

Roadmap work is organized by product theme first, then annotated with maturity and horizon labels. This keeps related work together without turning the roadmap into a long time-bucket list.

Initiative labels are planning signals, not release promises:

- `Maturity`: `ready`, `foundation-needed`, or `exploratory`.
- `Horizon`: `near`, `later`, or `deferred`.
